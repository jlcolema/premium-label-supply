Tips On Using the Templates Properly

  1. If your text is too large, it may print off center. Consider shrinking the size until it centers properly.
  2. In your word processor, you can turn on and off the Gridlines to see the labels. In Microsoft Word it’s as simple as clicking the ‘Layout’ tab in the top section, then ‘View Gridlines’.
