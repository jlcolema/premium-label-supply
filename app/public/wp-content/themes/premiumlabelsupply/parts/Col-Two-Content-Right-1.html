OUR STORY

Established in 2016, Premium Label Supply was founded on the idea of supplying the highest quality American made shipping labels to every size customer at the lowest prices available, as well as free shipping. We manufacture our own labels in-house at our facility located in Birmingham, Alabama using materials sourced from the industry leading paper mills. We are committed to delivering the absolute best experience to every customer, every time.
